About Be'Lovd

Baking Love Into Every Cake Since 2020

Our Story

Be'Lovd was born from a simple belief that every celebration deserves a cake that's as special as the occasion itself. Since 2020, we've been crafting premium cakes that celebrate life's sweetest moments.

What started as a small home bakery has grown into Mumbai's most loved Valentine's cake destination. Each cake is handmade by our expert pastry chefs using only the finest ingredients - Belgian chocolate, fresh fruits, pure vanilla, and premium dairy products.

We believe that great cakes come from great ingredients and even greater care. That's why every cake that leaves our kitchen is a labor of love, baked fresh to order and delivered with a smile.
